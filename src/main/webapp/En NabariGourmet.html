You can eat Nabari City's good cheap eats "Gyujiru" a beef soup with Iga beef while looking at the Takigawa River.

Eikichi

A shop that has been making kaitenyaki for 50 years in front of Muroji Temple.
Yomogi-kaitenyaki, a mugwort bun is grilled in the Imagawa-yaki style with plenty of red bean paste.
You can probably only eat it here, cheap at 100 yen per piece, and flaverful and delicious.
By the way, Muro-ji's parking lot is 600 yen, and Eikichi's parking lot is free,
so it's wise to park here when visiting Muro-ji and use the money saved to buy a Kaiten-yaki.

709-2 Muro, Uda-shi, Nara-ken 633-0421 Japan
