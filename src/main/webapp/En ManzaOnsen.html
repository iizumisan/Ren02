Yokote Sancho Hutte

A bakery located in the highest place in Japan at an altitude of 2300m.
To get there, required to change lifts and climb some light mountain.
Recommendation is bread with mushroom soup. You can enjoy hot soup with plenty of vegetables.
Borscht is also popular because it is hot and delicious with plenty of vegetables.
