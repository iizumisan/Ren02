Minpika

At a delicious shaved ice cafe on Hateruma Island, we recommend the Kuromitsu (black honey) special!
One of the appeals of this cafe is that you can feel the comfortable breeze while looking out at the ocean.
Please note that cafe is closed in case of rain due to the seats on the open terrace.
