MinamiAso

It is located in the northeastern part of Kumamoto Prefecture, in the southern part of Aso Caldera.
Most of MinamiAso consists of forest and wilderness.
High-quality agricultural products are produced due to the favorable climate, with a temperature difference
between day and night and an annual average rainfall about 2,300 mm.

Furano

Furano City is located almost in the center of Hokkaido. Furano-dake of the Tokachi-dake mountain range rises to the east,
Ashibetsu-dake of the Yubari Mountains rises to the west, and the University of Tokyo Forest is located to the south.
It is located in a natural environment with forests occupying about 70% of the city area.

GinzanOnsen

Ginzan Onsen is named after the Nobesawa Ginzan, which once prospered as a large silver mine in the early Edo period.
Western-style wooden hotels built from the late Taisho period to the early Showa period are lined up along both banks
of the Ginzan River, and you can enjoy the unique scenery of the old Japanese days.

Hateruma Island

Hateruma Island is one of the Yaeyama Islands in Okinawa Prefecture. It belongs to Taketomi-cho, Yaeyama-gun.
It is the southernmost inhabited island in Japan.
It has an area of ​​12.73 km2 and a population of 482 (as of the end of March 2021).

Ishigaki Island

It is an island that combines subtropical nature and the city in Okinawa.
While there are resort hotels and shopping malls, there are Okinawan red-tile houses and tropical flowers in the city area.
Peaceful scenery spreads out.

ManzaOnsen

Manza Onsen has long been popular as the inner parlor of Karuizawa.
Located at an altitude of 1800m, it is also the highest hot spring resort in Japan.

Kyoto

Kyoto, the ancient capital, is also famous for its many historical buildings, such as Kiyomizu Temple and Byodoin Temple.
There are about 300 nationally designated important cultural properties (buildings), which is the largest number in Japan.
It is one of leading tourist site in Japan, with tourists from all over the world visiting throughout the year for its seasonal scenery
such as autumn leaves and cherry blossoms.

Nabari

Nabari City is located in the Iga region of western Mie Prefecture.
Located in the southern part of the Iga Basin, it is surrounded by beautiful　mountains and valleys,
such as Akame Shiju Hachi Waterfall, and Kaochi Valley.
